## Key Decisions

### [M11-09] SPECIFICATION — WEAKENED doubling uses post-damage state
**Would have asked:** Should the WEAKENED check for acid duration doubling use the pre-damage state (like claw's infect_weakened) or the post-damage state?
**Assumption made:** Post-damage state. Unlike claw (which enforces a two-hit invariant for state transition), acid's WEAKENED bonus is a damage amplification — if a hit weakens an enemy, the acid DoT should benefit from that weakened state immediately. This makes acid reward accuracy/timing.
**Confidence:** High

### [M11-09] SPECIFICATION — Acid DPS finalized at 1.0
**Would have asked:** What is the appropriate DPS for acid? DEFAULT_ACID_DPS (0.2) is too low for a primary attack effect.
**Assumption made:** DPS = 1.0, yielding 3.0 total DoT damage over 3.0s (6 ticks × 0.5). Combined with 1.0 direct hit = 4.0 total per hit. Against WEAKENED (6.0s): 1.0 + 6.0 = 7.0 total. Meaningful but not lethal against 10 HP enemies.
**Confidence:** High

### [M11-09] SPECIFICATION — Direct hit damage set to 1.0
**Would have asked:** What should the acid projectile's direct-hit damage be?
**Assumption made:** 1.0 (low). Acid is DoT-focused (1.0 upfront + 3.0 over time = 4.0 total) vs claw's burst (3.0 instant). Different tactical identity: acid rewards patience, claw rewards precision.
**Confidence:** High
